Author Biography

Ansilo Boff grew up immersed in church life, where faith was not a weekend activity but the air he breathed. After years of ministry involvement, theological study, and spiritual experience … alongside a career in medicine and clinical research … he began to notice a growing tension between the God he was taught to fear and the Father Jesus revealed.

The 'Rediscovering Jesus' Subverted Teachings and the Father's Love' series is part of his ongoing work to re-examine Jesus' words with honesty, courage, and compassion, and to invite others into a faith freed from fear.

He writes to the beat of his own drum, refusing to soften his tone when his views run counter to long-held traditions about life, love, and the universe. He values evidence as well as allowing the imagination to run free, and he is passionate about helping others gain a deeper sense of hope and optimism … and the confidence that comes from knowing they are unconditionally loved, accepted, and one with God. He loves sunsets and magnificent expressions of art, relishes a good lateral-thinking puzzle and the occasional murder mystery, and is currently ahead on points in the battle against carbs.
